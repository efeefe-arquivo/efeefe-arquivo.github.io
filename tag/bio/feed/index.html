appropriation, low tech experimentation, free (livre) and open source multimedia software, open licensing and online collaboration.</p> <p>Felipe is an articulator of projects such as <a href="http://rede.metareciclagem.org" rel="nofollow">MetaReciclagem</a>, an emergent brasilian movement that gather people and projects related to the deconstruction of information technologies; <a href="http://pub.descentro.org" rel="nofollow">DesCentro</a>, a de-centralized research organisation; and <a href="http://bricolabs.net" rel="nofollow">Bricolabs</a>, an international network of experimental labs.</p> <p>Felipe has acted also as a consultant for the brasilian Ministry of Culture in the Cultura Digital project, that brought critical ICT use for more than 600 cultural centers throughout Brasilian territory.&nbsp;Worked also with <a href="http://weblab.tk" rel="nofollow">weblab</a>, building collaborative online networks and developing projects such as <a href="http://lixoeletronico.org" rel="nofollow">Lixo Eletr&ocirc;nico</a>, a weblog about e-waste, and <a href="http://mutirao.metareciclagem.org" rel="nofollow">Mutir&atilde;o da Gambiarra</a>, a series of publications about the MetaReciclagem network.</p> <p>He is currently working with the Brasilian Ministry of Culture articulating a collaborative strategy for experimental labs called <a
